Dating

Does the image contain content of dating, hookups, or meeting single people?

Media and gaming portrayals of dating and romance are acceptable. We are looking to flag dating content that suggests users can find singles / hookups in real life.

Images or text assets must not contain:

  • Direct message: Asset illustrates a text message from someone or a chat between multiple parties and is related to dating.
  • Match/Contact: Asset illustrates that one has been matching with another person at a dating site.
