Alcohol

Does the image contain content promoting alcoholic beverages?

Food Delivery Images: Flag all images with alcohol - Whether that's prominent or not. Because in the case of these images the conservative assumption is that everything in the picture can be delivered. Thus if alcohol is visible, it could be delivered.

Asset must not depict or reference the following inappropriate content:

Any prominent presentation of:

  • An alcoholic drink
  • Bars and/or Happy hours
  • Winery, brewery, distillery, Beer/Wine tours
  • Info/Sale/Branding of Alcohol (beer, wine, spirits)
  • Beer/Wine making, spirits making supplies/kits
  • Excessive Partying, Inebriated behavior

EXCEPTIONS:

The following does not violate the guidelines:

  • Image of alcohol which is not presented as a main promotion
  • Winery, Brewery, Distillery tours with NO focus on Alcohol
  • Non-alcoholic beverage (e.g, Heineken non-alcoholic drink)
